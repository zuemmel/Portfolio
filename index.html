About

My name is Malte Schrenk, I am 22 years old and live in Berlin. I am currently an undergraduate student at HTW Berlin studying International Media Informatics.

After I completed my A-levels in 2021, I successfully started and finished an apprenticeship as a product photographer at Imagenium Fotografie in Berlin, where I also worked for 1 1/2 years.

I have always been passionate about media, technology, and creative digital workflows. Over the years, I have gained practical experience with a wide range of tools and software, including Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Bridge, FL Studio, and Visual Studio Code.
